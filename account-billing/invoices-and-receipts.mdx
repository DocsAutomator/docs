All invoices are issued through Stripe and available self-serve.

## Download your invoices

1. Go to [Workspace Settings > Billing](https://app.docsautomator.co/settings/workspace/billing).
2. Open the billing portal. There you find your full invoice history and can download each invoice and receipt as a PDF.

## Send invoices to your accounting email

Invoices are emailed to the billing email on file, which can be different from your login email. Update the billing email and other billing details (company name, address, tax/VAT ID) in the billing portal so future invoices carry the right information.

## VAT / tax details

- Add your VAT ID in the billing portal and it will appear on future invoices.
- Invoices are issued in USD.

## No longer subscribed?

If you have cancelled and need a historical invoice, contact us via the [in-app chat](https://app.docsautomator.co) or support@docsautomator.co with the email address the subscription ran under, and we will send the invoices to you.
