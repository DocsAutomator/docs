DocsAutomator offers a **50% discount on all paid plans** for nonprofit organizations, NGOs, and educational institutions.

## Who qualifies

- Registered nonprofit organizations and NGOs (any country)
- Charities
- Schools, universities, and other educational institutions

## How to apply

1. Reach out via the [in-app chat](https://app.docsautomator.co) or email support@docsautomator.co.
2. Include a link to your organization's website (or similar lightweight proof of your nonprofit or educational status). A formal registration document is usually not required.
3. We create a personal discount code for your organization and send it to you in the same conversation.
4. Apply the code during checkout. It works for **monthly and annual** plans.

<Note>
The discount applies to your entire invoice: the plan subscription as well as usage-based charges such as overage documents and e-sign completions.
</Note>

## Good to know

- You can test DocsAutomator for free for as long as you need before subscribing. Test mode documents are unlimited and carry a watermark.
- If your checkout goes through without the code by accident, contact us and we will sort it out.
