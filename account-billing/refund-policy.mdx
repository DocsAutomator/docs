We keep refunds simple and fair.

## When we refund

- **Accidental purchases** -- you subscribed or upgraded by mistake and have not used the plan: full refund if you reach out within **14 days**.
- **Annual renewals** -- your annual plan auto-renewed and you did not intend to continue: full refund of the renewal if you reach out within **14 days** of the renewal charge.
- **Wrong plan by mistake** -- you picked annual instead of monthly (or a bigger plan than intended): we switch you to the intended plan and refund the difference.
- **Billing errors** -- double charges or charges after a confirmed cancellation are always refunded in full.

## When we usually do not refund

- Partially used subscription periods. If you cancel mid-period, your plan stays active until the end of the period instead.
- Usage-based charges for documents or e-sign completions that were actually generated.

## How to request a refund

Contact us via the [in-app chat](https://app.docsautomator.co) or support@docsautomator.co with the invoice number (see [Invoices & Receipts](/account-billing/invoices-and-receipts)). Refunds are returned to the original payment method, typically within 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

<Note>
Unsure whether your case qualifies? Ask us. We would rather look at it than have you charged for something you did not want.
</Note>
